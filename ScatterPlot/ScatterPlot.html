This is a scatter plot that plots total expenditure for fruit in a year on the y axis and codified income brackets on the x axis. The value for the x axis can be referenced with the legend on the right. You can change which year is loaded and what fruit is selected with the drop down menus to the right. You can hover over the individual bubbles and the red regression line to get more information.
Note:This graph shows at the same time around 50,000 families which would mean 50,000 individual bubbles. But most bubbles are not being shown due to them being covered up by other bubbles or many bubbles having the same income code and total expenditure. The vast amount of data also explains why this visualization loads to slowly.
