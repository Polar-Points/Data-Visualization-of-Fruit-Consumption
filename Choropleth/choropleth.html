This is a U.S. map that that shows the average price of fruit in a county. Deeper shades of blue indicates more expensive prices, and specific ranges of prices are shown in the legends. Additionally, each red bubble represents a county's average fruit consumption. The dataset is sliced by month; the time slider on the top left allows the user to see counties' monthly average price and consumption of fruit.
