Organic Fruit Data Visualization

Quyen Ha and Marty Dang
In Collaboration with Erik Nelson

Our project is to visualize American consumption and price patterns with regards to organic and inorganic fruits. Our main focus is to explore the relationship between consumption/price and various attributes such as income, average market price, geographical location, etc.

Our data, collected by the Nielson Corporation, consists of 60,000 U.S households that continually provided information on their purchases of organic and inorganic fruits from 2011 - 2013. We hope to see how various factors like race, income etc. affect organic fruit purchases.
